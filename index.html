Untha started long before it had a name.

It began with us — two girls who grew up side-by-side, sharing dreams, memories, and the quiet belief that one day, they would build something of their own.

What started as a childhood friendship turned into a vision, and that vision slowly became Untha.

UNTHA is a reflection of who we are — simple, real, and intentional. Every product, every detail carries a piece of our journey and the bond that brought us here.

We didn't just want to create something beautiful. We wanted to create something that feels like you — powerful, feminine, confident, and true.

— The Founders of Untha ✦
